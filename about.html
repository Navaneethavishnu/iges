Who we are to Serave the nation

As part of its larger mandate to develop human potential to its fullest, IIT Delhi launched an Initiative on Gender Equity and Sensitisation (IGES), on International Women’s Day - March 8, 2018.


IGES aims to foster growth and solidarity irrespective of sex, gender, caste, class, ethnicity, language, race, dis/ability and sexual orientation

IGES builds on the work initiated by Gender Awareness Committee (GAC) which was instituted in IIT Delhi in 2015 under the leadership of Prof. Sangeeta Kohli and Prof. Ravinder Kaur. The GAC has been organising workshops on gender sensitisation and awareness sessions on sexual harassment at workplace, with students and staff members.

The IGES unit looks forward to your contribution in shaping this new body by taking initiative and interest around gender and diversity issues.
